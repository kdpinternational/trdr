How It Works
Four rules.
No exceptions.
A trade that does not satisfy all four rules is rejected at registration. No workarounds. No edge cases.
01
Manual registration only.
Submit entry, SL, and TP manually. The system validates the live price, auto-calculates R:R, and publishes. No broker API. No automation. You register every trade yourself.
02
SL mandatory. Minimum 50 points.
No SL, no trade. Minimum 50 points — not pips. Eliminates spread-as-stop entries and noise. R:R is only meaningful when risk is real and defined.
03
Maximum duration: 48 hours.
Unresolved after 48h → auto-closed at current price. DOSSIER is a ranking for active day traders. Swing traders and position holders cannot compete here.
04
Your record is permanent.
Every trade, every loss, every drawdown. Public. Forever. You cannot delete a losing trade. That is precisely what makes the rank worth having.
Who This Is For
The rules filter
automatically.
Manual registration, 48-hour max, 50-point minimum stop. Three constraints that define exactly who belongs at the top of this ranking.
Filtered out
Swing & position traders
Trades held for days hit the 48-hour limit. A meaningful rank requires active, daily trading. The leaderboard rewards those who show up every session.
Filtered out
Robots & automated systems
No API. Every trade registered manually. Bots firing hundreds of entries per day cannot register them. Algorithmic trading has no place here.
Filtered out
Scalpers with noise stops
A stop below 50 points is rejected. Eliminates entries where risk equals the spread. Every registered trade reflects a real, deliberate decision.
What remains
Active day traders who perform.
Present every session. Deliberate about setups. Define risk before entering. Register every trade by hand. Trade often enough to build a rank that means something. If that is you — this was built for you.
