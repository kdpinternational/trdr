How it works.

Three steps. Nothing hidden.

Step 01

Traders publish real trades

Every trade is entered manually — Entry, Stop Loss, Take Profit. The moment it's published, it locks. No edits. No manipulation. Ever.

Step 02

We calculate Recovery Rate

Not profit. Not win rate. Only how well traders recover from losses. Calculated automatically. Cannot be faked.

Step 03

Users subscribe to top traders

Choose traders based on recovery — not hype. Pay monthly. Receive only real, locked trades. No promises. Just results.
